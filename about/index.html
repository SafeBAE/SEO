About Vibe Check

Vibe Check is a private reflection tool for moments of uncertainty — when you’re wondering whether a boundary may have been crossed or someone may have been hurt.

Instead of judging or labeling you, Vibe Check helps you slow down and think through what happened. It guides you step by step, helping you look at behaviors, reactions, and the context around them. Along the way, you’ll reflect on things that can shape how a situation is experienced, including consent, pressure, alcohol, miscommunication, and power dynamics.

Vibe Check doesn’t collect personal information, make legal decisions, or report anything. It’s a space to think honestly about a situation, understand it more clearly, and consider what responsibility and next steps might look like — with resources to support safer decisions.

Privacy

No personal data is collected. Vibe Check does not ask for your name, email, location, or any identifying information.

Nothing is stored. Your responses, reflections, and choices exist only in your browser while you’re using the tool. When you close the tab, everything is gone.

No personal data is sent anywhere. There are no accounts, no cookies, and no server. Your responses and reflections stay in your browser. We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to count page visits — it does not track individuals, use cookies, or collect personal information.

Nothing is reported. Vibe Check does not contact authorities, schools, employers, or anyone else. It is not a reporting tool.
